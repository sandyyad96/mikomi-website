Questions

Frequently Asked

Are these resources free?

Yes, completely free. Every resource in our Learning Center can be downloaded at no cost — no payment and no subscription required.

Can beginners use these?

Absolutely. Our Hiragana and Katakana workbooks assume zero prior knowledge and start from the very first character, with guided stroke order throughout.

Do I need to join a course?

No. These resources are free for everyone, whether or not you ever enrol with us. If you'd like structured guidance alongside them, our live classes are always open to you.

How do I know my JLPT level?

Most learners begin at JLPT N5. If you already know some Japanese and aren't sure where you stand, message us on WhatsApp — we'll help you identify the right level free of charge.
