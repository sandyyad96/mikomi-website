Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need prior knowledge of Japanese?

Not at all. Our N5 course starts from absolute zero — hiragana, katakana and basic grammar are taught step by step.

Are the classes online?

Yes. All classes are live and interactive, conducted online so you can learn from anywhere in India.

Will I receive study material?

Yes. Every student gets curated study material, worksheets, practice tests and recorded session access.

How long are the courses?

Each JLPT level typically runs 3–5 months depending on the batch pace and level.

Do you provide placement guidance?

Yes. We offer career counselling and placement guidance for Japanese MNCs, translation roles and study-abroad pathways.

Can working professionals join?

Absolutely. We run flexible weekend and evening batches designed for working professionals.

Is there a free demo class?

Yes. Book a free demo class and a free counselling session before you enrol.

Still have a question? Ask us on WhatsApp — we usually reply within minutes.
